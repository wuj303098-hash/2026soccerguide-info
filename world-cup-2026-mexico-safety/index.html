What Fans Should Know

Plan around official venues

Use official stadium, ticket, and transport information where possible. Keep hotel, route, and matchday timing decisions close to known venue locations.

Arrive early

World Cup crowds make transport, entry lines, and neighborhood traffic slower than normal. Give yourself extra time before kickoff.

Keep documents and tickets simple

Store digital tickets, identification, and hotel details where they are easy to access, and avoid relying on one phone battery for everything.
